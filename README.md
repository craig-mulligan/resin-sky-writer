##Skywriter to Pi Connection

You can use a full-sized Skywriter board with your Raspberry Pi and our library by mimicking the connections that the HAT users. They are as follows:

Skywriter  | Raspberry Pi
-----------|--------------
GND        | GND
TRFR       | GPIO 27 
RESET      | GPIO 17
SCL        | GPIO 3 / SCL 
SDA        | GPIO 2 / SDA
VCC        | 3V
